Best Practices & Multi-Factor Authentication

‚úÖ Strong Password Guidelines

  • Use at least 12 characters with mixed case, numbers & symbols
  • Avoid personal information, dictionary words, or common patterns
  • Use unique passwords for each account
Good Example: Coffee$Morning#2024!

‚ùå What NOT to do

  • Using a single password for everything?
  • Using your pet's name for a password?
  • Using your grandchild's name for your password?
  • Using 'password' for your password?
  • Using '123456' for your password?
  • Using your birthday for your password?
81% of data breaches involve weak or stolen passwords
